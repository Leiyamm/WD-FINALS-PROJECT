The Project

This project was assigned as our final submission to our professor, Sir Ian Ramirez. With some similarity to our midterm submission, this website one-ups the previous activity as it showcases the works we've done under the "Web Design with Client-Side Scripting" course. On top of that, this website features an "About Me" page (styled similarly to a resume), a "Contact" page, a showcase of all my previous projects, and a photo gallery containing photos of me, the developer.

Mission

To further enhance my skills as an IT student by practicing different programming languages, conceptualizing features in game development, and observing my seniors' work and taking inspiration from such. To develop not only websites but also connections with fellow developers, building a network of people that are in my field of study, spearheading projects that I can say I'm proud of 50 years from now.
